01 — About

A detail‑oriented engineer
who cares about craft.

I'm Thomas Li, a third‑year Software Engineering co‑op student at the University of Ottawa. I'm drawn to high‑impact systems — satellite ground software, DevOps platforms, AI developer tooling — and to the quiet design decisions that make them feel calm to operate.

My most recent co‑op was at the Department of National Defence with 3 Canadian Space Division, where I shipped full‑stack features in Java Spring Boot and React TypeScript, designed an end‑to‑end Playwright testing pipeline, and led an AI developer tooling adoption initiative — growing the program from one developer to seven.

Before that, I wrote embedded C and C++ at Telesat LEO on the Lightspeed satellite constellation. The numbers I'm most proud of: a 210× and an 8× FlashDB performance improvement, and a quiet ARM64 Protobuf bug fix that nobody asked for.
